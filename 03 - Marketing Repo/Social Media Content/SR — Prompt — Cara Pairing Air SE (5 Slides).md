## ═══ PROMPT ═══

**Reference:** Use the Saramonic design system (from system prompt) and extract the Air SE pairing steps from the **product manual I've uploaded**.

Create a **5-slide static image carousel** for Instagram (1080 × 1350 px, 4:5) and TikTok (1080 × 1920 px, 9:16). Deliver both formats for each slide.

---

### SERIES OVERVIEW

**Topic:** How to manually pair the Saramonic Air SE
**Tone:** Clear, instructional, pro-friendly — not tutorial-channel cringe. Short steps, no hand-holding.
**Visual language:** Clean, minimal. White or dark brand background. Product photography prominent on Slide 1, icon/graphic-led on Slides 2–4.

---

### SLIDE 1 — COVER

**Goal:** Stop the scroll. Establish topic immediately.

**Layout:**
- Top: Series label tag — small, uppercase — e.g. `HOW TO`
- Center: Product shot of Saramonic Air SE (TX + RX together)
- Bottom: Headline text block

**On-image copy:**
- Headline: `Cara Pairing Saramonic Air SE`
- Subhead: `Manual pairing in [X] steps` *(extract step count from manual)*

**Visual notes:**
- Product shot centered or slightly right-weighted
- Strong contrast between product and background
- Saramonic logo bottom-right

---

### SLIDES 2–4 — STEP-BY-STEP

**Goal:** Explain each pairing step clearly. One step per slide.

> Extract the **manual pairing steps** from the uploaded Saramonic Air SE product manual. Adapt the language — keep it short, direct, Indonesia-first. Max 2 sentences per step. No verbatim copy from manual.

**Layout (repeat for each step slide):**
- Top-left: Step number — large, styled — `01` / `02` / `03`
- Center: Icon or simplified product diagram illustrating the action (button press, LED indicator, device connection)
- Bottom: Step instruction text

**On-image copy per slide:**
- Step number + short action headline (≤ 6 words)
- 1–2 lines instruction, konkret — what to press, what LED to look for, what happens next
- Example format:
  ```
  01
  Nyalakan TX dan RX
  Tekan tombol power pada TX, lalu RX.
  Tunggu hingga LED berkedip cepat.
  ```

**Visual notes:**
- Consistent grid and text placement across all step slides — same margin, same step number position
- Use iconography or simplified line-art diagram — not a photo of hands pressing buttons
- LED indicator status (color) should be visually represented if referenced in the manual
- Saramonic logo bottom-right, same position as Slide 1

---

### SLIDE 5 — CLOSING / WATERMARK

**Goal:** Brand close. Reinforce identity. No product info, no CTA text.

**Layout:**
- Full brand background (primary or dark brand color — from design system)
- Center: Saramonic logo (large, light/inverted version)
- Below logo: Tagline

**On-image copy:**
- Tagline only: `Sound. Remarkable.`
- Nothing else — no hashtag, no CTA, no product name on the image

**Visual notes:**
- This slide should feel like a brand stamp — confident, clean, final
- Minimal elements — logo + tagline only
- High contrast: light elements on dark background (or reverse if design system specifies)

---

### CONSISTENCY REQUIREMENTS (ALL SLIDES)

- Same bottom-right logo placement across all slides
- Same font, same margin, same grid — slides must read as a cohesive set
- Step slides (2–4): identical layout structure — only content changes
- Color palette strictly from Saramonic design system — no variations
- No decorative elements outside the design system

---

### DELIVERABLES

For each of the 5 slides, deliver:
1. **Instagram version** — 1080 × 1350 px (4:5)
2. **TikTok version** — 1080 × 1920 px (9:16) with safe zone respected (top 200px, bottom 300px clear)

Total: 10 files (5 slides × 2 formats)
